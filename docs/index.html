✈️ Airplane Banner

An airplane flies a banner across your screen before each Google Calendar meeting — so you actually stop forgetting them.

Download for macOS Download for Windows

Why it’s nice

Impossible to miss

A full-width flyby beats a notification you’ll swipe away without reading.

Multiple accounts

Watch work and personal Google Calendars at once, and pick exactly which calendars fly.

Won’t embarrass you

Stays grounded while you’re presenting fullscreen or have a Focus / Do Not Disturb on.

Working hours

Only fly on the days and hours you choose — no banners at 2am.

Four banner styles

Retro Arcade, Aurora Glass, Sunset Pop, or Mono Minimal — with a live preview.

Click to join

Optionally click the banner to open the meeting’s video link. Per-meeting reminder times respected.

Daily agenda

The menu-bar icon lists the day’s remaining meetings at a glance.

Auto-updates

New versions download themselves in the background and install on quit.

Install

Grab the .dmg (macOS) or .exe (Windows) from the latest release. On macOS, first launch needs right-click → Open (the build isn't notarized). Calendar access is read-only.

How it works

Sign in with Google (read-only calendar scope), and Airplane Banner quietly watches your selected calendars. A few minutes before each meeting — you choose the offsets — a plane tows a banner with the countdown and title across whichever screen you’re looking at. Everything runs locally; nothing is uploaded.
